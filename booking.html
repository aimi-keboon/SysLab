Booking Terms & FAQ

Important information about booking, payments, and cancellations

What happens after I book my spot?

+

After booking, we will review your application to ensure you're eligible to join the program. If you're not eligible, we will return your booking fee in full. If you're approved, you'll receive confirmation and further details about the program.

When do I need to pay the remaining balance?

+

The remaining balance (80% of the total fee) must be settled at least one week before the program start date. If payment is not received by this deadline, your spot will be cancelled and the booking fee will be non-refundable.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

+

If you cancel more than one week before the program starts, you may be eligible for a refund (subject to review). If you cancel less than one week before the program, the booking fee is non-refundable.

What if SysLab needs to cancel or delay the program?

+

If we need to cancel or significantly delay the program due to circumstances on our end, you will receive a full refund of all payments made, including the booking fee.

Who is eligible for the program?

+

The program is designed for motivated individuals aged 18-40 including students, professionals, entrepreneurs, and business owners. No prior coding experience is required, but participants should be committed to active learning and building a working system.

What's included in the program fee?

+

All packages include 2 nights accommodation, all meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks), expert instruction, AI development tools access, course materials, and a certificate of completion. Higher tiers include additional personalized support and post-program assistance.
